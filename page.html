Let’s Introduce about
South Penghu Marine National Park

Taiwan's South Penghu Marine National Park, encompassing Dongyuping, Xiyuping, Dongji, and Xiji Islets, is a pristine marine area known for its clear waters, rich coral reefs, and diverse coastal landscapes. Influenced by natural forces and human activities, the coastal morphology here is dynamic, creating both beauty and navigational challenges. Our project uses Landsat and Sentinel-2 satellite imagery, along with ICESat-2 LiDAR bathymetry, to monitor and showcase changes in this environment from 1986 to the present. Through this platform, we aim to share the unique coastal evolution of this national park with a broader audience.
